About

This tool serves as a collaborative platform, facilitating interaction and cooperation among various users, including individuals, doctors, and pathologists, to enhance the provision of healthcare services. It offers a comprehensive solution for monitoring the health status of individuals, empowering doctors to deliver accurate prescriptions and recommendations through the Human Digital Twin (HDT) interface.
